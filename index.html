Mail Abbreviations

发表于 2016-03-03

缩略语是一种很有意思的语言形式。中文中存在，如“喜大普奔”，“然并卵”等等。英文中也有，以下是本人收集到的一些在邮件中常用到的缩略语。

No. Abbreviation Original
A AFAIK As Far As I Know
AFK Away From Keyboard
AKA Also Known As
ASAP As Soon As Possible
AWHF Are We Having Fun?
B B4 Before
BCNU Be Seeing You
BBL Be Back Later
BFN Bye For Now
BRB Be Right Back
BTW By The Way
C CU / CYA See You
CUL / CUL8R / CYAL8R See You Later
D DUR? Do You Remember?
F F2F Face To Face
FYI For Your Information
FWIW For What It’s Worth
H HTH Hope This/That Helps
I IC I See
ICWUM I See What You Mean
IMHO In My Humble Opinion
IMO In My Opinion
IOW In Other Words
J J4F Just For Fun
JK Just Kidding
L L8R Later
LOL Laughing Out Loud
N NRN No Reply Necessary
O OBO Or Best Offer
OBTW Oh, By The Way
OIC Oh, I See
OTOH On The Other Hand
P PMFJI Pardon Me For Jumping In
POV Point Of View
PS Postscript
PPS Post-Postscript
R ROFL / ROTFL Rolling On The Floor Laughing
RSN Real Soon Now
RTM Read The Manual
T TAFN That’s All For Now
TIA Thanks In Advance
TMTT Too Much To Type

TTFN Ta Ta For Now
TTYL Talk To You Later
TTTT To Tell The Truth
TX Thanks
TYVM Thank You Very Much
W WB Welcome Back
WRT With Regard To
WTG Way To Go
Z ZZZ Sleeping, Bored, Tired
2 2BCNTD To Be Continued
2nite Tonight
4 411 Information
4ever Forever
Others ^5 High Five!
